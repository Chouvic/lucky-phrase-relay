🧧 About Spring Couplets (春聯): Traditionally written on red paper with black or gold ink and hung on door frames during Chinese New Year. The right scroll (右聯 / 上聯) is read first, the left scroll (左聯 / 下聯) second, with the horizontal header (橫批) across the top — always read right to left!
